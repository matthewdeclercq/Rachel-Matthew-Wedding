Getting There

We’re getting married on Drummond Island, Michigan! It’s a beautiful place, but it’s a bit remote. Here’s how to get there:

  1. Navigate to the Drummond Island Resort.
  2. Access to the island is via the Drummond Island Car Ferry — just show up and drive on!
  3. Once you arrive, park your car and enjoy the weekend. No more driving required… or go explore, whatever!

Lodging

We have discounted room rates for our wedding weekend! Just follow these steps:

  1. Copy this group code: 25RANTAN.
  2. Go to the reservation site and book your accommodations.

Attire

We’re going for a vintage romance vibe. Rachel is the only one wearing white — for the rest of you, wear something nice (no ties required). Check out this lovely inspiration board.

Other Notable Items

Babies and kiddos are welcome! On the other hand, if you want a weekend away that’s cool too. The ball is in your court! Sidenote – the resort does not have cribs, cots, or childcare.

Resort rooms & cabins are pet-friendly. Let the resort know when making your reservation. Pet sitters on the island: Scott Ellis (231-881-7450) and LCI Pet Resort (906-484-2194). Kindly refrain from bringing pets to the ceremony or reception.
